Dice & Dares

A free multiplayer Truth or Dare party game

About the app

Dice & Dares is a mobile party game for playing Truth or Dare with friends in the same room. Add your players, roll the dice, and the game picks who goes next and whether they face a Truth or a Dare. It's designed for game nights, parties, and casual fun — no account required to play.

What you can do

  • Roll the diceThe Party Randomizer picks the player and challenge for you.
  • Truths & DaresBuilt-in prompt decks for different moods and group types.
  • Custom promptsWrite and save your own truths and dares.
  • AI generatorDescribe a theme and get fresh AI-generated truths & dares.
  • Multiplayer scoringTrack players, scores, and completed challenges.
  • Play Games sign-inOptional Google Play Games for achievements & leaderboards.
